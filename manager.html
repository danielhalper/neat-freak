How to use Neat Freak

  • Tidy tabs — click the Neat Freak icon in your toolbar, then "Tidy tabs". Your open tabs are saved as memory-light folders and the originals close.
  • Find anything — type in the search box to filter by keyword. Press ↵ to ask in plain English (e.g., "UX applicants I looked up").
  • Restore — click any saved tab to reopen it. Hover a folder or session to reveal the "open all" arrow.
  • Keep what you're on — by default Neat Freak keeps your current tab open. Toggle this in Settings or the popup's Options.
  • AI grouping — bring your own OpenAI key in Settings for workstream-aware folder names and smart search.
